Gaming has been apart of my life ever since i went to the library in my grandpa's village while there visiting,
and some dirtbag teenagers had brought CS1.5 with them and were 2v2ing to see who was the best,
ofcourse me and my brother were instantly intrigued. We watched them play for hours, captivated by the fast-paced action.
we pesterd them to let us play. It was a whole new world for us, and we wanted to be a part of it.
